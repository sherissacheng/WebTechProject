I'm an avid reader; I started in elementary school with popular fantasy series such as Harry Potter and Percy Jackson, and have since then moved into more realistic themes. However, magical realism holds a near spot to my heart, as it combines both the quirky and fun bits of fantasy while still staying grounded. Listed below are different tables representing my thoughts on books I have read.
