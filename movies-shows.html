Favorite Movies

Fallen Angels

by Wong Kar-wai

Fallen Angels is the second movie of Wong Kar-wai's I've watched and definitely sits near the top of my list of movies I've enjoyed. I particularly liked He Zhiwu: though mute, his actions are hilarious yet thoughtful, and he shows a lot of personality throughout the movie. I love how Cantonese wasn't the only spoken language; the Mandarin provided by He Zhiwu kept the dialogue fresh and interesting. I enjoyed seeing each character's position in life, though I did feel that they were hardly connected until the end.
