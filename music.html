Music has always been a huge part of my life! My mom used to be a piano teacher, so I played piano for a good part of my childhood until I quit to play the violin in middle school. I played the violin for a couple of years until I switched to the viola in high school, where I played in my school's Symphony Orchestra. We ended up placing first at Nationals in New York with our performance of Shostakovich's Symphony No. 10. Though I haven't touched an instrument in a while, I still have a deep appreciation for all kinds of music, as you'll find below.
