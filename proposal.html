Sherissa's Final Project Proposal

What is this website about?

This website is about me! I'll talk about my interests and hobbies and pretty much anything you can learn about me put into one site.

What's the objective of this website?

The objective of this website is for keepsakes - I want to memorialize my current self and the things I enjoy... what makes me, me!

Who is my target audience?

My target audience is anyone who is interested in learning about me and my interests (and Professor Li of course)

What content do I plan on including in my website?

The five tabs I plan on incorporating into this site include:

  • Books - my favorite books, books I don't like, pretty much anything I have read these past couple of years
  • Movies & Shows - similar to books, my favorite and non-favorite movies and shows, anything I've consumed recently
  • Music - again like books and movies/shows, as well as concerts I've been to
  • Food & Drink - I've slowly started becoming a "live to eat" person and learning how to cook (which isn't going very well to be honest)
  • Places I've traveled to - I traveled a lot when I was younger, so any memories and photos I can conjure up of places I've visited!
